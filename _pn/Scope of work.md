A scope of work (SOW) is an agreed-upon outline of the work you're going to perform on a project. It includes:

1. [[Deliverables]]
2. [[Milestones]]
3. [[Timelines]]
4. [[Reports]]
5. [[Scope]]

The SOW is a way of documenting [[SMART questions|SMART]] answers to all of the [[Question|questions]] you ask during the [[Ask]] phase of the project.
