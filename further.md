# Further Reading
The following resources have been immensely useful to me, both in preparing the DASH framework and with my data analytics career more generally. I hope that they will help you to expand on some of the ideas presented here.

## Books

- [Agile Data Warehouse Design](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Agile-Data-Warehouse-Design-Collaborative/dp/0956817203) by Lawrence Corr and Jim Stagnitto. DecisionOne Press, 2011.
- [#Makeover Monday: Improving How We Visualize and Analyze Data, One Chart at a Time](https://www.makeovermonday.co.uk/book/) by Andy Kriebel and Eva Murray. Wiley, 2018.

## Sites

- [Thinking Like An Analyst](https://mavenanalytics.io/course/thinking-like-an-analyst) by [Maven Analytics](https://mavenanalytics.io)
- [7 Fundamental Steps to Complete a Data Analytics Project](https://blog.dataiku.com/2019/07/04/fundamental-steps-data-project-success) by [Dataiku](https://www.dataiku.com)
- [How to create a measurement plan and why you really need one](https://www.freshegg.co.uk/blog/analytics/performance-measurement/how-to-create-a-measurement-plan-and-why-you-really-need-one/) by [Fresh Egg](https://www.freshegg.co.uk)
- [Digital Marketing and Measurement Model](https://www.kaushik.net/avinash/digital-marketing-and-measurement-model/) by [Kaushik](https://www.kaushik.net)

- [Super Data Science](https://www.superdatascience.com/podcast) by [Jon Krohn](https://www.jonkrohn.com)
